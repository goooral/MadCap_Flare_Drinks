Kvass/Gira

Lithuanian beverage made of bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 l water

  • 100 g rye bread, dried and toasted (lithuanian bread is strongly recommended)

  • 50 g sugar

  • 1 teaspoon yeast

Instructions

Combine ingredients

Cut the rye bread into small pieces and dry them in a toaster oven or a regular oven until they are crispy.

Bring the water to a boil in a large pot.

Add the dried bread to the boiling water and let it steep for 15-20 minutes.

Prepare the mixture

Remove the pot from the heat and let it cool completely.

Strain the liquid to remove the bread pieces.

Add sugar and yeast to the cooled liquid.

Stir well to dissolve the sugar.

Fermentation

Transfer the mixture to a clean, sterilized container.

Cover the container with a cloth or paper towel and secure it with a rubber band.

Let it ferment at room temperature for 1-2 days, or until it becomes bubbly and slightly acidic.

Bottle and store

Once the fermentation is complete, strain the kvass to remove any sediment.

Bottle the kvass and store it in the refrigerator.
